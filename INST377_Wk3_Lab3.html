Rock-Paper-Scissors Rules:

Game Description:

You know the deal. Paper covers Rock. Rock breaks Scissors. Scissors cuts Paper. Lock in.

Game Procedure:

1. Pick Rock, Paper, or Scissors
2. The computer randomly picks Rock, Paper, or Scissors as well
3. Click "Shoot!"
4. Whoever wins has a claim to the other's firstborn child (and Gotham City!)
